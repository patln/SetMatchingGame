Set Matching Game

A fun pattern recognition exercise!

How to play:

Find 3 cards whose 3 criteria are either matching or unique.

27 Unique cards have 3 criteria: shape, color, and number.

3 matching or 3 unique criteria make a set!

(e.g. All different shapes, colors, and numbers)
(e.g. 3 cards of 3-stars with different colors)
(e.g. 3 cards of gold stars with different numbers)
